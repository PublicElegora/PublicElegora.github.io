Request deletion via email fastest

If you can’t open email links on this device, write to support@YOUR-DOMAIN with subject “Account Deletion Request (Common)”.

How to request deletion

  1. Email us at support@YOUR-DOMAIN from the same email you used to sign up.
  2. Include your account email, phone number (if you provided one), and (if available) your in-app User ID.
  3. We’ll reply to verify your request. Once verified, we start deletion immediately.

Tip: If you currently have access to the app, you can also request deletion from the in-app support entry (Profile → Help). If you don’t have access, email is fine.

What gets deleted

  • Account & profile (name, display name, bio, avatar/profile photo).
  • Credentials & identifiers (email, phone, hashed auth identifiers).
  • Push notification tokens and device tokens.
  • Your created content in the consumer app: saved events, personal bookmarks/favorites, friend connections, comments/messages, call rooms/metadata you initiated.
  • Your creator/business data tied to the account (business profile, event drafts, non-approved event submissions).
  • Images and files you uploaded that are tied solely to your account.
  • Location data associated with your account (e.g., last known map region, preferences).

What may be retained (minimal)

  • Security & operational logs (e.g., server access/error logs) for up to 90 days to detect abuse and ensure platform reliability.
  • Backups that may contain your data until they automatically expire and are overwritten, typically within 30 days.
  • Legal/financial records (if any payments or tax documents exist on your account) for the period required by law (often up to 7 years).
  • Aggregate analytics that do not identify you (e.g., counts and statistics where individual records are irreversibly de-identified).

Timeline

  • Start: Immediately after we verify the request belongs to you.
  • Completion: Within 7 days for active databases and storage.
  • Backups & logs: As noted above, backups typically purge within 30 days; security logs within 90 days.

We use cascading deletes in our backend so that once your account is removed, associated personal data linked to it is also removed from our active systems.

Identity verification

To protect accounts, we verify every deletion request. We may ask you to confirm:

  • Access to the email address on file (reply verification), and/or
  • Recent sign-in details or non-sensitive account information (e.g., display name).

If you no longer have access to the sign-in email, contact us and we’ll provide alternate verification steps.

Content that involves other users

If your activity produced content that other users depend on (e.g., public event posts that became part of community listings), we remove personal identifiers and, where feasible, delete or anonymize the content. If any public image or post still identifies you after deletion, email us and we will remove it.

Contact

Email: support@YOUR-DOMAIN

If you’re writing from a different email than the one on your account, mention the account email in your message so we can locate it.
