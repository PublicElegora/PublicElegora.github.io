Developer: Elegora (as shown on our Google Play store listing)

Request deletion via email

If the button doesn’t open an email client, write to commonDoReply@gmail.com with the subject “Account Deletion Request (Common)”.

How to request deletion

  1. Email commonDoReply@gmail.com from the same email you used to sign up.
  2. Include your account email, phone number (if you provided one), and (if available) your in-app User ID.
  3. We’ll reply to verify your request. Once verified, we start deletion immediately.

If you still have access to the app, you can also request deletion through the in-app support entry (Profile → Help). If you don’t have access, email is fine.

What gets deleted

  • Account & profile (name, display name, bio, avatar/profile photo).
  • Credentials & identifiers (email, phone, hashed auth identifiers).
  • Push notification tokens and device tokens.
  • Your created content in the consumer app: saved events, personal bookmarks/favorites, friend connections, comments/messages, and call rooms/metadata you initiated.
  • Your creator/business data tied to the account (business profile, event drafts, non-approved event submissions).
  • Images and files you uploaded that are tied solely to your account.
  • Location/preferences associated with your account (e.g., last map region, filters).

What may be retained (minimal)

  • Security & operational logs (e.g., server access/error logs) for up to 90 days to detect abuse and ensure reliability.
  • Backups that may contain your data until they automatically expire (typically within 30 days).
  • Legal/financial records (if payments/tax records exist) for the period required by law (often up to 7 years).
  • Aggregate analytics that do not identify you (irreversibly de-identified statistics).

Timeline

  • Start: Immediately after we verify the request belongs to you.
  • Completion: Within 7 days for active databases and storage.
  • Backups & logs: Backups typically purge within 30 days; security logs within 90 days.

We use cascading deletes so that once your account is removed, associated personal data linked to it is removed from our active systems.

Identity verification

To protect accounts, we verify every deletion request. We may ask you to confirm:

  • Access to the email address on file (reply verification); and/or
  • Recent sign-in details or non-sensitive account information (e.g., display name).

If you no longer have access to the sign-in email, contact us and we’ll provide alternate verification steps.

Content that involves other users

If your activity produced content that other users depend on (e.g., public event posts that became part of community listings), we remove personal identifiers and, where feasible, delete or anonymize the content. If any public image or post still identifies you after deletion, email us and we will remove it.

Contact

Email: commonDoReply@gmail.com

If you write from a different email than the one on your account, mention the account email so we can locate it.
